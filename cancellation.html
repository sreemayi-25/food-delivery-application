Refund and Cancellation Policy for Craving Kitchen

Refund Policy:

  1. Craving Kitchen strives to ensure utmost customer satisfaction with every order. However, if you encounter any issues with your order, we offer a refund policy to address your concerns.

  2. Refunds will be processed under the following circumstances:

    1. Incomplete or incorrect delivery of the ordered items.

    2. Quality issues with the delivered food.

    3. Order cancellation due to unavailability of items.

    4. Payment discrepancies or overcharges.

  3. To initiate a refund request, customers must contact our customer support team within [insert time frame, e.g., 24 hours] of order delivery or within a reasonable time frame of issue occurrence.

  4. Refunds will be processed to the original mode of payment within [insert time frame, e.g., 3-5 business days] upon approval of the refund request.

  5. In case of payment made via cash on delivery, refunds will be provided through suitable alternative methods coordinated with the customer.

Cancellation Policy:

  1. Customers can cancel their orders without any cancellation fee provided the cancellation is made before the order is confirmed by the restaurant.

  2. Once the order is confirmed and under preparation, cancellations may not be feasible. However, customers can contact our support team to explore possible options.

  3. For cancellations made after the order is confirmed but before delivery, applicable charges may be levied depending on the stage of order preparation and restaurant policies.

  4. Customers are encouraged to review their orders carefully before confirming to avoid any cancellation charges.

  5. Craving Kitchen reserves the right to cancel any order in case of unforeseen circumstances such as unavailability of items, technical glitches, or any other reasons beyond our control. In such cases, customers will be notified promptly, and refunds will be initiated as per our refund policy.

Additional Terms:

  1. Refunds and cancellations are subject to the discretion of Craving Kitchen and may vary depending on individual cases and circumstances.

  2. Any promotional discounts or offers applied to the order will be adjusted accordingly in case of refunds or cancellations.

  3. Customers are encouraged to provide accurate contact information to facilitate smooth communication regarding refunds and cancellations.

  4. For any queries or assistance regarding refunds or cancellations, customers can reach out to our customer support team via cravingkitchen@gmail.com

Please note that this policy is subject to change, and any updates will be communicated to our customers through appropriate channels.
