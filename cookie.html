What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device (computer or mobile device). They are sent by a website or mobile application when you visit or use it. Cookies are stored in your device's browser or mobile application and help the website or application remember information about your visit, preferences, and actions over time.

  • Understand how you use our Service.
  • Monitor and analyze the performance, operation, and effectiveness of our Service.

  2. Analytics and Performance Cookies:

  4. Functionality Cookies:
