About Us

Craving Kitchen is a leading online food ordering and delivery platform that connects customers with their favorite local restaurants. Our mission is to make it easy for peopleto enjoy great food from their favorite local restaurants, wherever they are.

We offer a wide range of features to help customers discover and order food from their favorite local restaurants, including real-time order tracking, easy payment options, and fast and reliable delivery service. We are committed to providing the best possible experience for our customers, and we are constantly working to improve our platform and expand our offerings.

Founded in 2024, Craving Kitchen has quickly become one of the most popular food delivery platforms in the country, with a growing network of over 200+ restaurants and a loyal customer base of millions of users. Our platform offers a wide variety of cuisines, from Indian to Italian, Chinese to Mexican, and everything in between. At Craving Kitchen, we are committed to supporting local restaurants and helping them grow their businesses. We believe that by connecting customers with great local food, we can help build stronger communities and support the local economy. We are constantly innovating and improving our platform to make it even easier for customers to discover and order their favorite foods, and for restaurants to reach new customers and grow their businesses.
